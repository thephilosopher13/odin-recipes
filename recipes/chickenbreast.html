Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Herb-infused chicken dish made with large, bone-in chicken breast halves. Rubbed with olive oil and garlic and sprinked with herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 large bone-in chicken breast halves with skin
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and garlic.
  3. Sprinkle chicken with salt, black pepper, rosemary and basil.
  4. Arrange chicken in a large baking dish.
  5. Refrigerate chicken for at least 45 minutes.
  6. Preheat your oven to 190 decrees C (Or 165 degrees F if you're a seppo).
  7. Bake chicken in preheated oven until the chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear (this takes around 45-60 minutes). The thickest part of the breast meat should have a temperature of around 75 degrees C (or 165 degrees F for seppos).
  8. Serve hot and enjoy.
