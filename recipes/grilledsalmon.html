Grilled Salmon

Description

Marinated fish that is sweet and savory as it is seasoned with lemon pepper, garlic powder and salt. A marinade of soy sauce, brown sugar, water and vegetable oil is then added in order to introduce more flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1/2 lbs) salmon fillet(s)
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder and salt.
  2. Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water and vegetable oil together in small bowl until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Place fish in large resealable plastic bag.
  4. Add soy sauce mixture, seal the bag and turn to coat.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  6. Preheat an outdoor grill (medium heat) and lightly oil the grate.
  7. Place salmon on preheated grill and discard marinade.
  8. Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with fork (6-8 mins per side).
  9. Enjoy!
